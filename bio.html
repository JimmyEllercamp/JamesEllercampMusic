James Ellercamp is a Screen Composer | Producer based between Sydney, Australia and Toronto, Canada. Whilst his stylistic diversity is vast, he specialises in contemporary, particularly Indie | Rock | Ambient music.

James has written for many different projects and clients, from tv advertisements, film, promos, to producing music for other artists, and most recently the Lofi Indie project GAL.

James has a history of performing and playing various different contemporary small ensemble intstruments. Projects that he has worked on through soundhouses TA2 studios and Eggplant Collective include clients such as Mitsubishi, Canadian Tire, and Budweiser, as well as private clients which include The Word On The Street, The Creats, Rebecca Diem and others.
